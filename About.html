Need for Speed


Project for IT-214 Fundamentals of Software Development, Sec-1 by Dr. Michael Hart, Fall 2019.


Project RC-Car is a full fletched remote-control car which utilizes our very own android app as a controller and can also use any basic Arduino Bluetooth App. The car uses power from a rechargeable 12V power source to power Arduino Mega 2560 and L293D Adafruit Motor Shield plus its libraries for desired movement control through an HC-06 Bluetooth Module. The Car also has a HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor connected at the front part for it to avoid any obstacle present in front.
